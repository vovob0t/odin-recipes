The potatoes with canned meat stew

Ingredients

  1. Vegetable oil
  2. Onion ~~ 1.5 pc
  3. Carrot ~~ 1.5 pc
  4. Bell pepper ~~ 2 pc
  5. Garlic ~~ 3-4 cloves
  6. ...any vegetables you want to add
  7. Potatoes ~~ 6-7 medium pc
  8. Tomato paste - 2 tbl. spoons
  9. Canned meat - 1 pc
  10. Salt, black paper, paprika, oregano, other spices for your taste
  11. Hot Sauce or Red Pepper Flakes (optional)
  12. Water ~~ 3-4 cups of boiling water
